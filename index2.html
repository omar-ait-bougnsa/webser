About the service

IMG WORLD OF ADVENTURE DUBAI

IMG World of Adventure is one of Dubai’s best theme parks, bringing Marvel and Cartoon Network characters to life with state-of-the-art rides. It is also the world’s biggest indoor theme park which means that no matter what kind of weather, you can always visit IMG World of Adventure without worrying about the weather.

IMG World is good for all, whether you like Adventure or need a family time out. We‘re bringing a special offer for you with an exceptional discount for couple and family booking.

Avenger lovers, Good news! This is a place for you.


What's included?

  • Entry or admission fee
  • Parking fees
  • Full-Day Access to IMG World of Adventure
  • Unlimited Rides

Important Info: Entrance for children under 1.05-meter height is free

The ticket is valid for both Adults and kids.


Exclusions

  • Food & drinks
  • Retail items (available to purchase)
  • Souvenir photos (available to purchase)
  • Lockers are available at the park with additional charges


Age Policy

Adult Height 1.1 meter and above
Child Under 1.1 meter (FREE)


Timings

Sunday - Thursday 12:00 PM - 10:00 PM
Friday & Saturday 12:00 PM - 11:00 PM


Ticket Validity

The tickets are flexible and valid for the next 5 - 6 months from the purchase date, so you can visit anytime at your convenience. This ticket is valid for both, adults & kids.


Please note

  • Public transportation nearby
  • Disabled Guests must enter the theme park on a full-priced day ticket & need to provide proof of disability, one helper can gain entry to the theme park for free with them.
  • No strollers are allowed inside the vehicles without prior information so please inform us at the time of making the reservation.
  • A valid photo ID is mandatory to gain entry to the park.
  • Rides will operate subject to availability.
  • Children below 1.05 m get to enter for free. Child tickets (Between 1.05 m - 1.19 m) and senior citizen tickets are available at reduced prices.
  • Children must be accompanied by parents/guardians at all times.
  • Outside food and drinks are not allowed on the park premises.
  • Smoking within the park premises is not permitted.
  • During the Holy Month of Ramadan, the activity will not have any Live Entertainment or House Beverages.
  • Convenient storage lockers are available at the park entrance.
  • The Haunted Hotel is only open to guests aged 15 and older.
  • No Pets allowed
